Type of lease

Select operating lease, if you wish to return the vehicle upon expiry of the agreement. As a rule, we can offer you this type of lease, if the seller is a partner of SEB Liising and provides redemption guarantee for the residual value of the vehicle.

Select financial lease, if you wish to pay the entire cost of vehicle during the validity term of the lease agreement and become the owner of the vehicle upon expiry of the agreement.

Select financial lease without VAT, if you purchase a used vehicle from a private individual or a company, which is not liable to VAT. You will become the owner of the vehicle upon expiry of the lease agreement.
